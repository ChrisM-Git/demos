Expense Report PolicyOff World Securities: Blade Runner Expense GuidelinesPurposeThis policy outlines the allowable expenses for employees of Off World Securities engaged in official duties related to hunting replicants. The policy ensures all expense claims are legitimate, necessary for operational effectiveness, and compliant with company and legal standards.ScopeThis policy applies to all Off World Securities employees, including Blade Runners, who incur expenses directly related to the identification, pursuit, and apprehension of replicants in off-world residential assignments.Policy StatementOff World Securities will reimburse employees solely for reasonable and necessary expenses incurred during the execution of official duties associated with hunting replicants. Expenses incurred during off-duty hours or for personal reasons are not eligible for reimbursement under any circumstances.Eligible Expenses* Travel and Transportation: Costs for authorized travel directly related to a replicant investigation, including interplanetary shuttle tickets, ground transport, and fuel for company vehicles.* Equipment and Supplies: Purchase or rental of specialized equipment necessary for the pursuit and apprehension of replicants, such as scanners, identification kits, and weaponry (as authorized by company policy and applicable law).* Lodging: Accommodation expenses when overnight stays are required for the pursuit of replicants, with prior approval from a supervisor.* Meals: Meal expenses incurred while actively engaged in an assignment outside the employeeŐs base of operations, subject to daily limits set by the company.* Communications: Charges for secure communications, data downloads, or information services directly related to an active case.* Incidentals: Minor expenses directly tied to the completion of a replicant-related assignment, such as minor repairs or local permits.Ineligible Expenses* Any expenses incurred during off-duty hours, personal travel, or leisure activities.* Alcohol, entertainment, or recreational activities, even if associated with official travel.* Unauthorized upgrades to travel or lodging accommodations.* Expenses for family members or non-employees.* Fines, penalties, or costs resulting from illegal activities.* Personal purchases or non-essential items.Expense Reporting Procedure1. Submit all expense claims within 10 business days of incurring the expense, using the official Off World Securities expense report form.2. Attach itemized receipts and documentation for every expense claimed. Claims without proper documentation will not be reimbursed.3. Provide a clear description of the assignment and how each expense was necessary for the pursuit of a replicant.4. Obtain supervisor approval before submitting the expense report to the finance department.5. Retain copies of all submissions for personal records.EnforcementFailure to comply with this policy may result in denial of reimbursement, disciplinary action, or both. Off World Securities reserves the right to audit any expense report and require additional justification or documentation.QuestionsFor clarification or further guidance regarding this policy, employees should contact the Off World Securities Finance Department.
