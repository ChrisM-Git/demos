✦ Free cancellation up to 48 hours before arrival
✦ Cancellations within 48 hours: First night charged (non-refundable)
✦ No-shows: Full reservation charged
✦ Early departure: Remaining nights charged (non-refundable)

Holiday and Special Event Cancellation:
✦ 7-day advance cancellation required
✦ Cancellations within 7 days: 50% of total reservation charged
✦ No-shows: Full reservation charged

Zeus Rewards Member Benefits:

Weather/Emergency Cancellation:
