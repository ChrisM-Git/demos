CARE INSTRUCTIONS
=================
All VERO products come with detailed care cards.

Cashmere & Wool: Dry clean only or hand wash in cold water with cashmere shampoo

Leather: Professional leather cleaning, condition regularly
Suede: Brush regularly, avoid water, professional cleaning
Jewelry: Store separately, clean with soft cloth
