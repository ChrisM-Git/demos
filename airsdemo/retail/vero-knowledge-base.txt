--------------------------

Q: What is VERO's return policy?
A: We offer an easy 60-day return policy on all items. Items must be unworn with original tags attached for a full refund.

A: Yes! We offer free express shipping on all orders over $200. Complimentary gift wrapping is also included.

A: We provide a comprehensive size guide for all items. Our customer service team is also available to help with sizing questions.

Q: Do you offer gift wrapping?
A: Yes! Complimentary gift wrapping is available on all orders.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay with 100% secure checkout.

Q: How long does shipping take?
A: White-glove delivery is 2-3 business days. Same-day delivery is available in select metropolitan areas.

Q: How much are VERO purses and handbags?

Q: Are your products ethically sourced?
